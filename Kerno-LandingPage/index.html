What Kerno makes possible

Kerno focuses on the essential steps of a B2B marketplace: helping users build a trustworthy profile, browse supplier products, and start clear business conversations through structured requests.

Supplier profile → Publish products → Store searches → View details → Send request

Guided profile completion

Stores and suppliers are guided toward a complete professional profile. A circular completion gauge gives instant feedback and helps users understand how ready their profile is before they start using the marketplace.

  • Profile setup
  • Completion gauge
  • Trust signals

Supplier product catalog

Store users can browse products published by suppliers, explore details, categories and pricing information, then identify the offers that match their sourcing needs.

  • Product cards
  • Categories
  • Pricing units

Structured requests

Stores can send structured contact or quote requests to suppliers. Each request keeps the conversation tied to a product or business need, making the first contact clearer and easier to track.

  • Contact request
  • Quote flow
  • Status tracking

Why we built Kerno

It started with a frustration one of us kept running into: finding the right supplier is harder than it should be.

Walk into any independent shop and ask how they source their shelves. The answer is almost always a patchwork — a cousin who knows a producer, a business card from a fair two years ago, a folder of emails, a name overheard at a market. Good suppliers stay invisible, and good stores waste hours rediscovering what someone nearby already knew.

Meanwhile the suppliers we spoke to had the opposite problem. They had real products and genuine local value, but no simple, professional place to be found — no clean way to say “here is what we make, here is how to reach us.”

Kerno is our answer to that gap. Not another sprawling procurement suite, but a focused first step: help the right store find the right supplier, understand what they offer, and send one clear request. We kept the scope honest so the thing we shipped actually works.
